Detailed Description

Manages game's view into the world and all the world space drawing.

Handles transformations from world space to screen space and vice versa before drawing. The world space is a cartesian plane with Y-axis up for positive and down for negative. But, the screen space has the Y-axis flipped because computers work from top left to bottom right and the origin is the top left of the screen.

All draw functions (CURRENTLY) take world space positions which represent the CENTER of the thing being drawn.

Manages zoom level clamped to [ m_minZoom and m_maxZoom ] at all times. Zoom is applied to all draw functions.

◆ screenToWorld()

Vec2f Camera::screenToWorld ( Vec2f  screenPosition) const

Converts a screen space position to a world space position.

Converts the screen position by offseting the screen origin from top-left to center, removing zoom, flipping the Y-axis, remove the camera offset.

This is an inverse of worldToScreen.

Useful for stuff like knowing what a mouse click on the screen maps to in the world, based on where the camera is centered.

Parameters
screenPositionScreen space position to convert
Returns
Vec2f Converted world space position
